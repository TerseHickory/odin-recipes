Cream Cheese Garlic Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 6 small dinner rolls
  • 8 oz softened cream cheese
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 1 tbsp dried parsley
  • 1 large egg (room temperature)
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp crushed garlic

Steps:

  1. Cut rolls (not all the way)
  2. Combine softened cream cheese and white sugar
  3. Mix softened butter, parsley, egg, mayo, tsp sugar, and garlic in a bowl
  4. Put cream cheese micture in a ziplock bag. Pipe it to the bread (in between)
  5. Brush garlic butter sauce on top and sides of bread
  6. Bake at 350F for 15-20 min. Or at 320F for 6-7 min if using an airfryer
