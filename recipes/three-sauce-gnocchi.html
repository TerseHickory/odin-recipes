Three Sauce Gnocchi

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 potatoes
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup flour
  • Basil
  • Pine nuts
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Fresh tomato
  • Bay leaves
  • Sugar
  • Crushed red pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil potatoes until al dente
  2. Once potatoes cooked, mash in a bowl, sprinkle salt, add 1 egg, and mix
  3. Slowly add flour until a dough is formed
  4. Boil Gnocchi in salt water. Once Gnocchi rises to the surface, it is ready
  5. Mix basil, pine nuts, garlic, olive oil, parmesan, salt, and pepper in a food processor to make Pesto sauce
  6. Stir garlic, butter, heavy cream, and parmesan in saucepan to make cheese sauce
  7. Stir garlic, tomato, basil, bay leaves, crushed red pepper, salt, pepper, and sugar in a pot to make marinara sauce
  8. Separate gnocchi and plate up with different sauces
